# App Maintenance

Partner is fully responsible for application maintenance. This means providing and supporting the technological infrastructure reliable and performant enough to fully support the application use by Merchants.

## Maintenance Requirements

Application once deployed and published in the SmartPOS Store shall be regularly updated due to:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Security Requirements">
    Especially GDPR and data protection compliance must be maintained at all times.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Legal Constraints">
    Ensure compliance with applicable regulations and legal requirements in all markets where your app is available.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Operating System Updates">
    Terminal system updates (Android) may require application updates.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="SmartPOS Ecosystem Updates">
    Updates to WPI and SmartPOS API may require application updates.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Business Responsibilities

Besides technological maintenance, Partner shall fully cover business relations with Merchants using their application, which means:

- Full customer support
- Responsibility for commercial communication
- Responsibility for operational communication

<Warning>
WL is not responsible for such activities and obligations under any circumstances.
</Warning>

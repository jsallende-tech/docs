# What's New

Here you can find the latest information on page updates and new content added to the Partner Center space.

## Latest Updates

### Dec 19th, 2025: SmartPOS Available for UK!

Just before the end of 2025, as promised, Worldline launches SmartPOS in the UK! Thus, this will be the 17th country in which we are present with our new services for Android terminals.

### Oct 20th, 2025: SmartPOS Available in Nordic Countries

Finally! We are proud to announce SmartPOS services availability for Integrators in Nordic countries. **Denmark**, **Sweden**, **Norway** and **Finland** are now officially in SmartPOS family! We did integrate SmartPOS environment on Ingenico **Axium DX8000** payment terminal for these markets.

And this is not our last word!

### Sep 30th, 2025: SmartPOS Continues to Grow!

Next batch of countries have just arrived! From now the SMB markets in **Slovakia**, **Liechtenstein**, **Luxembourg**, **Austria**, **Hungary** and **Cyprus** are a part of SmartPOS Ecosystem for **Castles Saturn 1000** standalone payment terminal.

We do hard work to address more soon! Stay tuned.

### Jun 11th, 2025: SmartPOS Commercially Launched!

We are happy to announce that Worldline SmartPOS services has been officially launched! We addressed first markets like **Switzerland**, **Belgium**, **Poland**, **Slovenia**, **Czech Republic** and **Croatia** with SmartPOS services available on **Castles Saturn 1000** (S1F2) standalone payment terminal enhancing business solution for Small and Medium Businesses!

More countries soon!

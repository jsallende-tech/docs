# Application Upload in Developer Center

Once the signed app is delivered by WL Partner Management, it is ready for deployment.

## Upload Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Developer Center">
    Log in to [SmartPOS Store Developer Center](https://smartpos.whatspos.com/developer/).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Binary">
    Upload the signed binary file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Out App Information">
    Fill out the associated online form providing:
    - App description
    - Screenshots
    - Icons
    - App type and character
    - All relevant information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for Approval">
    Submit your application for approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>

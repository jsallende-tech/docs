# Value Added Services

Worldline SmartPOS ecosystem supports a variety of Value Added Services (VAS) that can enhance your merchant solutions.

## Available Services

  <Card title="Loyalty Programs" icon="star">
    Integrate loyalty and rewards programs with payment transactions

  <Card title="Receipt Management" icon="receipt">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Inventory Integration" icon="boxes-stacked">

  </Card>

## Getting Started with VAS

To implement Value Added Services in your SmartPOS application:

1. Contact your Worldline Partner Manager to discuss available services
2. Review the specific VAS documentation for your target services
3. Implement the integration following provided guidelines
4. Test thoroughly in the sandbox environment

<Note>
Availability of specific Value Added Services may vary by market and terminal configuration. Contact your Partner Manager for details.
</Note>

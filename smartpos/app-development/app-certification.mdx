# App Certification

The certification process for an application involves a series of steps and evaluations conducted to verify that the application meets specific security standards and guidelines.

## Compliance Standards

Your application must comply with:

- **Worldline Standards**: Internal security and quality guidelines
- **GDPR**: General Data Protection Regulation
- **PCI DSS**: Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard

## Certification Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prepare Documentation">
    Provide a [Product Delivery Note (PDN)](/smartpos/app-deployment/app-signature) along with the APK for each new release.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Non-Disturbance Testing">
    Your application undergoes Non-Disturbance Testing (NDT) to ensure it doesn't interfere with payment operations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="PDN Review">
    The Product Delivery Note is reviewed and approved by the Worldline team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Signing & Publication">
    Once approved, Worldline will sign the application and publish it on the Terminal Management System (TMS) or SmartPOS Store.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
This process ensures that the application has undergone rigorous security checks and is compliant with relevant standards before being made available to users.
</Info>

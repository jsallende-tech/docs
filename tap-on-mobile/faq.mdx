# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What devices support Tap on Mobile?">
    Tap on Mobile works on NFC-capable smartphones:
    - **Android**: Devices running Android 9+ with NFC
    - **iOS**: iPhone 7 and later running iOS 12+
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What card types are supported?">
    Tap on Mobile supports contactless payments from major card schemes including Visa, Mastercard, and others depending on your region and acquirer setup.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is Tap on Mobile PCI compliant?">
    Yes, Tap on Mobile solutions are designed with PCI compliance in mind. Contact your Partner Manager for compliance documentation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need special hardware?">
    No additional hardware is required - Tap on Mobile uses the smartphone's built-in NFC capabilities.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I test without a physical card?">
    Use the sandbox environment with test card numbers provided by your Partner Manager.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
